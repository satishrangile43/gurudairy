WHY WE BUILT GURUDIARY

Teachers spend hours calculating attendance, managing lost papers, and recreating timetables. We realized that existing apps force schools to buy expensive software or steal user data by keeping it on cloud servers.

GuruDiary was born with a single mission: To give individual teachers a powerful, 100% offline, private digital console. Your data, your device, your control.
